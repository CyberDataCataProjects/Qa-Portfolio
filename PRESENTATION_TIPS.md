# Presentation Tips for Interviews

- Start with the Test Plan: show scope and objectives.
- Walk through 1-2 test cases live, explaining preconditions and expected behavior.
- Demonstrate how you'd reproduce and prioritize a bug; show the BUG_REPORT_TEMPLATE.md example.
- Mention next steps: automation, CI, reporting, metrics.
- Be ready to explain trade-offs and testing heuristics used.
